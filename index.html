Short Bio

I am Assistant Professor at the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia. Before that, I was a postdoctoral associate at MIT CSAIL, working with Prof. Michael Stonebraker. I received the PhD degree in Computer Science from the University of Modena in 2016. My doctoral dissertation won the PhD Thesis Award from the IEEE Computer Society Italy Section. I held visiting positions at the DB group of the Univ. of Michigan and the Data Analytics group of the Qatar Computing Research Institute. My research interests include data integration and big data management.
